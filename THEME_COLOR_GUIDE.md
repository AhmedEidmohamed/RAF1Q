## 🎨 Color Accessibility

All colors are chosen for:
- ✅ WCAG AA contrast compliance
- ✅ Readability in light mode
- ✅ Readability in dark mode
- ✅ Color-blind friendly
- ✅ Child-friendly (soft, therapeutic)

### Contrast Ratios:
- Text on background: 7.5:1 (AAA standard)
- Text on card: 9.0:1 (AAA standard)
- UI elements: 4.5:1+ (AA standard)
